Credit Policy

This document outlines the core evaluation criteria used in the NB36 demo to assess applications. The policy is presented for demonstration purposes only and is not a binding agreement or a substitute for legal and compliance review.

1. AML (Anti‑Money Laundering)

We perform AML screening to identify potential sanctions, PEP matches, or other compliance red flags. Outcomes are expressed as PROCEED, REVIEW, or KO.

  • Watchlist and sanctions checks (mocked)
  • Basic identity verification alignment (mocked)

2. Fraud

We assess device, behavioral, and identity risk to detect potential fraud attempts. Outcomes are expressed as FRAUD_PASS, REVIEW, or KO.

  • Device fingerprint and velocity signals (mocked)
  • Behavioral and identity consistency checks (mocked)

3. Credit

We incorporate bureau tiering and scorecard‑like indicators to estimate creditworthiness. Outcomes are expressed as CREDIT_PASS, REVIEW, or KO, with a final_tier.

  • Tier derivation and minimum thresholds (mocked)
  • Review/KO reasons for transparency (mocked)

4. Income

We verify income via payroll/bank modes and assess coverage and stability. Outcomes are expressed as INCOME_PASS, REVIEW, or KO.

  • Coverage months and overage thresholds (mocked)
  • Source used (payroll/bank/document) (mocked)

Final Eligibility

The policy composes these signals to derive an overall outcome. In this demo, the modal displays a per‑check breakdown with timing and a final request identifier. Technical details provide request/response JSON for reference.
